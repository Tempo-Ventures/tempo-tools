3. Install in Gmail (Google Workspace)

  1. Click Copy signature above.
  2. In Gmail, click the ⚙️ gear (top-right) → See all settings.
  3. Stay on the General tab and scroll to Signature.
  4. Click + Create new, give it a name (e.g. "Tempo"), then click into the editor box.
  5. Paste with ⌘ + V (Mac) or Ctrl + V (Windows). The logo, fonts, and links should appear.
  6. Under Signature defaults, use the two dropdowns to assign the signature to your address — select Tempo for both For new emails use and On reply/forward use. If you have multiple addresses, repeat for each one.
  7. Tick the checkbox "Insert signature before the quoted text in replies, and remove the '--' line that precedes it."
  8. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Changes.
  9. Compose a test email to yourself to confirm it looks right.

LinkedIn link: enter your personal LinkedIn profile URL in the form above — it will be included directly in the signature.
